I Am Truly Sorry, My Angel ❤️

I have been thinking about everything… and my heart feels heavy.

I never meant to hurt you. That was never my intention.
Seeing you upset because of me hurts more than I can explain.

I know I am not perfect. I know I make mistakes.
But please believe me when I say — my heart is always with you.

I wanted to give you something beautiful…
something that would make you smile.
Instead, I made you feel hurt — and that breaks me.

If I could take the pain back, I would.
If I could show you how deeply sorry I am, I would do it a thousand times.

Please forgive me, my angel.
You mean more to me than I sometimes know how to show. ❤️
